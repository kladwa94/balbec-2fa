SMS Multi-Factor Authentication Enrollment

Balbec Capital LP employees enroll in SMS-based multi-factor authentication by providing their mobile number and consenting to receive one-time authentication codes from the IT department.

Program Overview

About This Program

Balbec Capital LP uses SMS-based Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) to secure access to internal systems. This program is for Balbec Capital employees only and is not open to the general public.

Employees who enroll receive one-time passcodes (OTP) via SMS, generated by Microsoft Azure, for authentication purposes only. No marketing messages are ever sent through this program.

Who can enroll

Authorized Balbec Capital LP employees who provide their own mobile number and consent to receive SMS codes.

How enrollment works

The employee submits their number and opts in through the form below. Enrollment is voluntary.

Message frequency

OTP codes are sent only when an employee attempts to authenticate. No recurring or scheduled messages.

Message & data rates

Standard message and data rates may apply based on the employee's mobile carrier plan.

Enrollment Process

How Employees Enroll

  1. 1

    During onboarding, the employee visits this page and enters their own mobile number, then checks the consent box to opt in to SMS authentication codes.

  2. 2

    After submitting, the employee receives a confirmation SMS: "You're enrolled in Balbec Capital LP's SMS MFA program for secure system access. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help."

  3. 3

    Upon login, the employee receives an OTP code via SMS: "Balbec Capital LP: Your verification code is XXXXXX. Valid for 10 minutes. Do not share this code. Reply STOP to opt out."

  4. 4

    The employee enters the OTP code to complete authentication. Codes expire after 10 minutes and are single-use only.

Opt-Out & Consent

Opt-Out Instructions

Employees may opt out of receiving MFA SMS messages at any time:

Reply STOP to any MFA SMS message to immediately unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-out requests are processed immediately. Employees who opt out will need to use an alternative MFA method (such as the Microsoft Authenticator app) to access company systems. To re-enroll, complete the form above or contact the IT Help Desk.

This program complies with CTIA messaging guidelines. Balbec Capital LP will never use this number to send marketing communications.

Privacy & Data

Data Use & Privacy

Mobile numbers collected through this program are used solely for authentication purposes. Numbers are stored securely in the company's MFA system and are never shared with third parties or used for marketing.

Enrollment records — including employee name, email, mobile number, consent timestamp, and registration date — are retained by the IT department in accordance with Balbec Capital LP's data retention policy.

For questions about data use, contact the IT Help Desk at support@balbec.com.
