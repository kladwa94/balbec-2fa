SMS Multi-Factor Authentication
Enrollment Policy

This page describes how Balbec Capital LP employees are enrolled in the company's SMS-based multi-factor authentication program managed by the IT department.

Program Overview

About This Program

Balbec Capital LP uses SMS-based Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) to secure access to internal systems. This program is managed exclusively by the IT department and is not open to the general public.

Enrolled employees receive one-time passcodes (OTP) via SMS generated by Microsoft Azure for authentication purposes only. No marketing messages are ever sent through this program.

Who is enrolled

Only authorized Balbec Capital LP employees whose numbers have been registered by an IT administrator.

Who manages enrollment

Enrollment is performed exclusively by Balbec Capital IT administrators — employees do not self-enroll.

Message frequency

OTP codes are sent only when an employee attempts to authenticate. No recurring or scheduled messages.

Message and data rates

Standard message and data rates may apply based on the employee's mobile carrier plan.

Enrollment Process

How Employees Are Enrolled

  1. 1

    During employee onboarding, the IT administrator registers the employee's mobile number in the company's MFA system as part of mandatory security access setup.

  2. 2

    The employee receives an SMS notification confirming their enrollment: "You have been enrolled in Balbec Capital LP's SMS-based MFA program for secure system access. Reply STOP to opt out. For help, contact IT at support@balbec.com."

  3. 3

    Upon login, the employee receives an OTP code via SMS: "Balbec Capital LP: Your verification code is XXXXXX. Valid for 10 minutes. Do not share this code. Reply STOP to opt out."

  4. 4

    The employee enters the OTP code to complete authentication. Codes expire after 10 minutes and are single-use only.

Opt-Out & Consent

Opt-Out Instructions

Employees may opt out of receiving MFA SMS messages at any time using any of the following methods:

Reply STOP to any MFA SMS message to immediately unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-out requests are processed immediately. Employees who opt out will need to use an alternative MFA method (such as the Microsoft Authenticator app) to access company systems. To re-enroll, contact the IT Help Desk.

This program complies with CTIA messaging guidelines. Balbec Capital LP will never use this number to send marketing communications.

Privacy & Data

Data Use & Privacy

Mobile numbers collected for this program are used solely for authentication purposes. Numbers are stored securely in the company's MFA system and are never shared with third parties or used for marketing.

Enrollment records including employee ID, registration date, and acknowledgment are retained by the IT department in accordance with Balbec Capital LP's data retention policy.

For questions about data use, contact the IT Help Desk at support@balbec.com.
