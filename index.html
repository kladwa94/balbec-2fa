About This Program

How SMS Two-Factor Authentication Works

Balbec Capital LP requires all authorized staff to enroll in multi-factor authentication (MFA) as part of our information security policy. Once enrolled, our system will send a one-time passcode (OTP) to your registered mobile number each time you sign in.

🔐

Verification Codes Only

You will only receive OTP codes for login authentication. No marketing messages, ever.

📱

Standard Rates Apply

Message and data rates may apply based on your carrier plan.

🚫

Opt Out Anytime

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance.

Consent & Opt-In

Your Explicit Consent is Required

Before enrolling your mobile number, please review the following consent statement carefully:

"By providing your phone number and checking the box below, you agree to receive one-time passcode (OTP) SMS messages from Balbec Capital LP for authentication purposes only. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for support. This number will not be used for marketing communications."

This consent is collected at the point of MFA enrollment and is logged with a timestamp, employee ID, and IP address for compliance purposes. Consent records are retained in accordance with Balbec Capital's data retention policy.
