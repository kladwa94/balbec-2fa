Privacy Policy

How Balbec Capital LP handles information collected through the SMS MFA enrollment program

Last updated: June 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Balbec Capital LP ("Balbec," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects personal information provided by employees who enroll in our SMS-based Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) program at this enrollment page. This program is for authorized Balbec Capital employees only and is not offered to the general public.

2. Information We Collect

When an employee enrolls, we collect the following information that the employee voluntarily provides:

  • Full name
  • Balbec Capital email address
  • Mobile telephone number
  • Consent status and the date/time consent was given

3. How We Use Information

Mobile numbers and related information are used solely to deliver one-time passcodes (OTP) for authentication when an employee signs in to Balbec Capital systems. We do not use this information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to authentication.

4. SMS Messaging

OTP messages are sent only in response to an authentication attempt. Message frequency varies based on the employee's login activity. Message and data rates may apply based on the employee's mobile carrier plan. Employees may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message, or reply HELP for assistance.

We do not share, sell, or rent mobile numbers or any information collected through this program to third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. No mobile information is shared with third parties for marketing.

5. Data Sharing

Information is processed through Microsoft Azure and Twilio solely to deliver authentication messages on Balbec Capital's behalf. These providers are contractually limited to using the information only to provide the messaging service. We do not otherwise disclose this information except as required by law.

6. Data Retention & Security

Enrollment records are stored securely within Balbec Capital's MFA system and retained in accordance with the company's data retention policy. Access is restricted to authorized IT personnel.

7. Your Choices

Employees may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP, or by contacting the IT Help Desk to be removed and to use an alternative MFA method such as the Microsoft Authenticator app.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your information, contact the Balbec Capital IT Help Desk at support@balbec.com.
